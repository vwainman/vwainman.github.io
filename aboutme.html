My name is Vincent Wainman, and that's my pup, Lily. I've recently graduated from the University of Alberta with a BCom in Management Information Systems and a minor in computing science. I am fascinated by information systems and their pivotal role in revolutionizing business processes. With data-driven decision-making being crucial in today's highly competitive business environments, I want to be at the forefront of uncovering insights to steer organizations in the right direction. My proficiency with information systems, programming, analytics, machine learning, and statistics makes me an ideal entry-level candidate as a data analyst. I am confident I can connect successfully with various stakeholders by bringing clarity to the problems that matter and delivering reports and presentations that translate information effectively.

I am looking for opportunities to pique my curiosity, challenge my technical abilities, and broaden my creative problem-solving skills. If we meet in person, you will notice my odd posture and limp. I had a benign astrocytoma tumor in my thoracic spine that caused severe scoliosis and nerve damage. I've been through several surgeries and quite a bit of physical rehabilitation over the years, and I'm happy to say that I'm stable now. I don't expect it to interfere with any of my work, but I'm an open book about it if you'd like to know more.
